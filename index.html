JOSTUM TECH FEST

Know more about JOSTUM TECH FEST

JOSTUM TECH FEST 2024 is a one day event aimed at equipping students with technological skills, bridging the tech education gap and fostering a tech-academia ecosystem. The JOSTUM TECH FEST is a 5 hour event featuring keynote speakers, panel discussions, networking opportunities, free consultation with a Microsoft Student Ambassador and the creation a new functional tech community with free resources. We have a target audience of over 500 students from Joseph Sarwuan Tarka University and other universities around including young adults. We aim at improving student experience, fostering collaboration and networking amongst students and industry experts, providing access to resources and certifications from Microsoft, Harvard online school and others, for learning.

What we aim to accomlish

The aim of Jostum Tech Fest:

  • Operation End Unemployment amongst University Graduates: To equip students with certifications from the created tech community and resources for employability, ensuring they are well prepared for the wider world beyond the degree they receive in the university.
  • Bridging the Gap: To bridge the tech education gap and ensure young adults and students evolves with the tech ecosystem, staying relevant and competitive.
  • Tech-Academia Integration: To enable students maximize technology in their studies and apply it to their chosen course, enhancing their learning experience.
  • Tech Inclusion: To bring tech education and functional tech communities to tech- deficient areas, promoting equal access to tech opportunities.
